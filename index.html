About Me

Hello and Welcome! I will try to give you insight into the whirlwind of ideas in my mind.

I'm a husband and father of 4 (1 on the way). I decided to convert my basement into a reptile den. We have 5 snakes, 6 geckos, 2 bearded dragons, and 2 tortoises. Maybe too small of a number to be called a den?

I LOVE to fiddle and tinker around with things. It drives my family crazy! I love to learn new things! I enjoy wondering around the hardware store and just looking at stock on the shelf and imagine the projects I could do. I'm a big gearhead, and love anything with a motor on it. On my birthday my wife suprised me with a track day. I was able to drive around the track in a Ferrari 488 GTB, with a professional driving instructor. WOW what a cool experience.

I have been pretty steadily going to college since 2011. I used to be a mechanical engineering major. After too many credits and years, I was prompted by family to switch to computer science. I loved it! I was in my element, finacially became too overwhelming. I felt like I was paying too much money to not getting my hands fiddling on some code! I have a huge drive to learn, I then went on months learning anything from the internet. I went down a route of learning Node.js, Angular, Swift, ios development, and many others. One of my roles I was part of the Business Intelligence department. I learned a lot of practical experience of how data should be organized and stored. I still have dreams about ER diagrams.

tl;dr I'm husband and father of 4. I love to code, tinker, solve puzzles and learn. I have snakes and other reptiles. I love anything with a motor on it (I currently have a diesel truck).
